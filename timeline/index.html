Timeline

  1. July 2016

    Lai becomes controlling shareholder

    He takes a 88% stake and takes over the £3.7m loan previously borrowed by ex-chairman Jeremy Peace.

  2. May 2018

    Relegation from the Premier League

    Despite their heavier spending in comparison to prior seasons, WBA are relegated from the Premier League.

  3. June 2022

    £4.95m loan

    In the 2020/21 financial accounts, Lai took out a loan for £4.95 million with 50k interest. This loan had a self-imposed deadline to be repaid by September 2021, however, it was not met.

  4. November 2022

    Action For Albion is formed

    Action For Albion was created in response to the events outlined in this timeline.

  5. December 2022

    £20m loan taken out

    MSD Holdings granted WBA a £20m loan for football operations. In total, WBA must pay back 28m, including interest.

  6. January 2023

    Lai Fails to Repay £5m Loan on Two Occasions

    Lai has failed to repay a loan on two occasions. Fans were told it would be paid in the new year, however the loan has grown to £5m and remains unpaid.
